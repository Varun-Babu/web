Construction Manager App

Streamline site management and boost productivity

Why Is This App Needed?

Managing multiple construction sites involves handling a lot of data, communication, and progress tracking. Most traditional approaches rely on manual updates, WhatsApp messages, and paperwork, leading to delays and errors.

Key Benefits

  • 📷 Upload site progress photos in real time
  • 📊 Automatic daily/weekly reports for clients
  • 📍 Track projects based on client and location
  • 🧑‍💼 Assign work, get instant updates from field engineers
  • 📁 Manage drawings, material logs, and approvals digitally

How It Works

  1. Project Creation: Add project details, clients, and team members.
  2. Daily Updates: Site engineers upload photos and updates directly from the field.
  3. Client Access: Clients view live updates from their mobile app.
  4. Reporting: Generate reports with just one click, saving hours of manual work.
