FAQ

Is NextBrick free?

Yes — project management and marketplace listings are completely free. We only charge for optional upgrades like Verified badges, promoted listings, and in-app ads.

How do I get Verified?

Upload your business documents, license, and sample project photos. Once our team reviews and approves, your profile will receive a Verified badge and priority visibility.

What is the benefit of being Verified?

Verified companies appear at the top of searches, get priority leads, and build trust with clients. This increases your chances of winning projects and growing faster.

Can homeowners or clients use NextBrick?

Yes. Clients can browse companies, check portfolios, request quotes, and compare builders, architects, or vendors all in one place.

What types of companies can join?

General contractors, architects, interior designers, suppliers, and other construction-related service providers are welcome to register on NextBrick.

How do ads and promotions work?

You can boost your profile visibility with promoted listings or targeted ads. These appear in featured spots across the marketplace and help you get more leads when needed.

Can I showcase my past projects?

Yes. You can upload photos, descriptions, and milestones of completed projects to build a portfolio that clients can view before contacting you.

Which areas does NextBrick cover?

Initially, NextBrick focuses on regional markets in India. Companies can list their city or service areas so clients can find local providers easily.

How does NextBrick connect clients and companies?

Clients search or browse companies on the marketplace, view their profiles and projects, and directly send requests for quotes or services through the app.

Do I need to install anything?

No. NextBrick is a mobile and web app. Simply sign up, create your company profile, and start managing projects or connecting with clients.
