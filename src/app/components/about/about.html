About V4 DevLabs

V4 DevLabs is an early-stage product startup building practical software for the civil & transport industries. Our flagship product, NextBrick, is a marketplace that connects clients with vetted builders and service providers — fast, transparent and reliable.

Task tracking Attendance Expense logs Project dashboards

NextBrick also includes a free project-management module for companies to track work, attendance, expenses and progress — enabling small and mid-sized firms to manage projects without heavy tooling costs.

We also operate V4 Code Academy campus hubs where B.Tech students learn by building: students design apps, complete certification sprints, and access internship pathways into product teams.

Our Vision

Create a trusted digital marketplace that connects clients with quality builders, and provide builders with lean tools to plan and scale work. Simultaneously, make engineering education project-first so graduates are ready to ship products from day one.

We believe better tools and better-trained people will reduce rework, improve timelines, and raise career outcomes for students entering the workforce.

Our Mission

Build two complementary systems: a marketplace that reliably matches clients with builders, and a practical, no-cost project-management platform that companies can use to run projects, log expenses and track attendance.

Through V4 Code Academy hubs, we train students to contribute to product development and accelerate their transition into internships or product roles within months of completing a hub sprint.
